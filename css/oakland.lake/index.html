Oakland lake, Nevada

Oakland lake is one of the most breathtaking attractions located in Oregon, Nevada. It is a home to a number of ski resorts, summer outdoor activities and recreations as well as tourist attractions. Hitchhikers, snowboarders and snow all over are a significant part of the lake's area reputation.
